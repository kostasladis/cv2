Short Bio

A marine industry veteran with deep cross-segment experience, Kostas started out as a bunker trader during his college years, where he gained a BA in Economics and a Masters degree in Shipping and Transport. While holding key positions in ship finance, operations and chartering, Kostas also added a Masters in Maritime Law. Returning to the bunker industry, Kostas is Sales & Purchase Manager for B&L TRANSOIL (HOLDINGS) LTD in Athens, Greece, where he leads business development and counterparty credit risk assessment.
